League Rules & Format

West GTA Clublink Men's Invitational — 2026 Season

Welcome! As the first whiffs of freshly cut grass have started wafting in the air, I am sure we are all looking forward to hitting the courses. This year, let's put a different spin on things.

The West GTA Clublink League Invitational will be a months-long event culminating in a 27-hole championship. This is NOT a Clublink sanctioned event.

Season Play Rules

Required Rounds (7 total)

Each player must play and record a round at each of the following courses:

  • 1× Georgetown Golf Club
  • 1× Blue Springs Golf Club
  • 1× Glendale Golf Club
  • 1× Greystone Golf Club
  • 1× RattleSnake Point – Sidewinder
  • 1× RattleSnake Point – Copperhead
  • 1× Heron Point Golf Links

Bonus Rounds (top 3)

After counting the best round from each required course, the next 3 best rounds from any course also count towards your total. These bonus rounds can come from any of the required courses above or from the bonus-eligible courses below:

  • + Hidden Lake Golf Club – New Course
  • + Hidden Lake Golf Club – Old Course
  • + Glen Abbey Golf Club
  • + Glencairn Golf Club – Leithfield-Speyside
  • + Glencairn Golf Club – Scotch Block-Leithfield
  • + Glencairn Golf Club – Speyside-Scotch Block

No rounds are required at the bonus-eligible courses — they only count toward bonus scoring.

  • ✓ All scores must be recorded in Golf Canada to count
  • ✓ Gross scores only
  • ✓ Rounds from any tee are eligible — no tee requirement
  • ✓ Multiple scores can be submitted — only the best scores will count
  • ✓ All scores to be submitted to westgtaclinvitational@gmail.com
  • ⚠ All scores must be submitted before August 1st to count towards the championship

Championship – August 3rd, 2026

Location: Glencairn Golf Club

Team Seedings

Teams of 4 golfers will be decided after season scoring is finished, based on the following seeding structure:

Team Seed 1 Seed 2 Seed 3 Seed 4

Championship Format – 27 Holes

Once teams of 4 are made, teams must decide on pairings for the championship round. The championship includes 3 × 9-hole rounds:

Round 1 9-Hole 2-Man Scramble
Round 2 9-Hole 2-Man Alternate Shot
Round 3 9-Hole 2-Man Better Ball (Handicapped)

The champion team will be the lowest scoring team after all 27 holes.
